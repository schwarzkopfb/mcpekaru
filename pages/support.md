# Support

If Kifli for ChatGPT is not working as expected, please report the problem through
the project's [GitHub issue tracker](https://github.com/schwarzkopfb/mcpekaru/issues).

## Include in your report

- What you asked the app to do.
- What happened instead.
- The approximate time of the problem.
- Any error message shown by ChatGPT.

Do not include passwords, access tokens, payment details, or other sensitive
information.

For questions about an order, delivery, refund, product, or Kifli account, contact
[Kifli support](https://www.kifli.hu/oldal/kapcsolat).
