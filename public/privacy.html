Kifli for ChatGPT

Privacy policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Kifli for ChatGPT lets an authenticated user search Kifli products through ChatGPT.

Information processed

The app processes product searches and product references sent through its tools. It also validates authentication data supplied by ChatGPT through Auth0. The app does not ask for or process payment card details.

How information is used

Information is used only to authenticate access, request matching product data from Kifli, and return results to ChatGPT. Search requests are sent to Kifli's product APIs as needed to provide the requested result.

Storage and sharing

The app does not maintain its own user profile or persistent history of tool requests. Information may still be processed by ChatGPT, Auth0, Kifli, and the hosting platform under their respective policies.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the project's GitHub issue tracker without including personal or sensitive information.
