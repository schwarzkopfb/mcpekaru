Kifli for ChatGPT

Find Kifli groceries with ChatGPT

Kifli for ChatGPT helps you search the grocery products available on Kifli.hu and inspect product details during a conversation.

What it does

  • Searches Kifli's current product catalogue.
  • Returns concise product information and links to Kifli.
  • Keeps access private through your authenticated account.

Purchases are made from Kifli. Product availability, prices, delivery, and customer support for orders are handled by Kifli.
